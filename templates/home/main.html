Session at MAX: HTML5 for ActionScript 3.0 developers

HTML5 Django Python

The MAX conference is approaching, happening on May 4-8th in Los Angeles and I will be giving a talk entitled HTML5 for ActionScript 3.0 developers , which I am super excited about.

I will be covering the differences and similarities between today and tomorrow and ActionScript 3, the browser APIs and solutions you want to use to power expressive content across desktop and mobile, and the options available to profile your content and optimize performance.

If you have been developing with ActionScript for years and you are diving into web standards to create expressive and interactive stuff, then I hope to see you there! For those of you who cannot attend MAX, the session will also be recorded and posted here.
